Getting to the Radisson Blu Hotel, East Midlands Airport

Address:
Radisson Blu Hotel
East Midlands Airport
Herald Way, Pegasus Business Park
Derby DE74 2TZ, United Kingdom

✈️ By Plane

The hotel is located directly at East Midlands Airport (EMA). It's a short 5-minute walk or a 2-minute shuttle ride from the terminal.

🚆 By Train

Nearest stations:

  • East Midlands Parkway – approximately 15 minutes by taxi
  • Derby Station – approximately 25 minutes by taxi

Regular train services are available from major cities across England, Wales, and Scotland.

Check National Rail train times

🚌 By Coach

National Express coaches operate services to East Midlands Airport from various UK cities. The hotel is within walking distance or a short shuttle ride from the coach stop.

🚗 By Car

The hotel is easily accessible from the M1 motorway (Junction 24). On-site parking is available.
