By Plane

The hotel is located directly at East Midlands Airport (EMA). It is a short 5-minute walk or a 2-minute bus ride from the terminal to Pegasus Business Park

By Train

Regular train services are available from major cities across England, Wales, and Scotland. Check National Rail train times

Nearest stations:

  • East Midlands Parkway – approximately 15 minutes by taxi
  • Derby Station – approximately 25 minutes by taxi

By Coach

National Express coaches operate services to Derby and Nottingham from various UK cities. View National Express coach routes and times

By Skylink

Skylink is a bus service operating from Derby and Nottingham to East Midlands Airport. The services have a stop at the Pegasus Business Park.The venue is a 2 minutes walk from the bus stop.

Once you access the skylink website, remember the following steps:

  1. choose your route: skylink express, skylink Derby or skylink Nottingham.
  2. choose your direction: From Derby or Nottingham towards the East Midlands Airport
  3. choose your stop: Pegasus Business Park - Radisson Blu Hotel.
